About Me

Hello!

Intro

As a Visual and Digital Product Designer, I’m always enthusiastic about exploring beyond the conventional, challenging preconceptions, and iterating according to discovery. Designing for mobile and web has encouraged me to blend problem-solving, research, and imagination to enhance the human experience. I’m also passionate about influencing positive change, and I hope that I can contribute to improving inclusivity and accessibility through our everyday digital interactions. View my project reel below!

Experience

I recently graduated with an Honours Bachelor degree in Design from York University, and in April, completed my second co-op term as a Visual Designer with the Royal Bank of Canada. I also had the pleasure of sharing my passion with my friends and peers as this year’s Co-President of the Design Students’ Association for the second year in a row.
