About Me

Hello!

Intro

As a Visual and Product Designer, I’ve always been enthusiastic about exploring beyond the conventional, challenging preconceptions, and iterating according to discovery. Designing for mobile and web has encouraged me to blend problem-solving, research, and imagination to enhance the human experience — without sacrificing the aesthetics. I’m also passionate about influencing positive change, and I hope that I can contribute to improving inclusivity and accessibility through our everyday digital interactions. To see some of my work, check out my project reel below!

Experience

I’m currently working as a Junior Visual Designer at ServiceTitan where I manage and extend design systems across four brands. There, I collaborate with developers to ensure seamless implementation of designs and partner with stakeholders to meet business objectives and requirements. I also recently graduated with an Honours Bachelor of Design from York University. Alongside my studies, I completed two co-op terms as a Visual Designer with RBC Bank and had the pleasure of sharing my passion with my peers as Co-President of the Design Students’ Association. Our team photo (captured by John Sanchez) is featured below!

Tools & Interests

On the technical side, I've been able to familiarize myself with Figma, Adobe CC, HTML, CSS, and (a little bit of) Javascript.

During my free time, I find pleasure in exploring plant-based restaurants, experimenting with new recipes, and volunteering! I also love connecting with new people, so feel free to say hi :)
