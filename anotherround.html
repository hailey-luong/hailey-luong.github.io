Another Round
Photo Catalogue

Overview

Featuring a selection of photographs, this catalogue captures the intricacies and craftmanship of shot glasses collected from around the globe.

Timeline

2020

Process

Forms

To capture the forms and details of the shot glasses, I began by experimenting with cutting out silhouettes on black paper and roughly illustrating the details in white. Although this provided for an intriguing manner to study the items, I realized that it was limited by the inability to reflect the textures and imperfections.

Photographs

I determined that photographing the objects would be the best approach to effectively emphasize the intricacies of each item. In total, I captured over 40 different designs in varying contexts using the Canon Rebel T3i. I curated a selection of those images below.
